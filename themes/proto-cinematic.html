02 · How I think

Three decisions I'm proud of saying no to.

Most PM portfolios brag about results. This one brags about judgment — the signal recruiters actually screen for at senior levels.

✕ Said no

3D LiDAR for v1.

Hardware team argued for robustness. I cut it. 2D + ultrasonics covered our actual envelope at one-third the BOM. Saved ~₹2.4L/unit.

✕ Said no

L&T's bespoke payload request.

Procurement wanted per-site customisation. Held to three standard trims. Two of three now account for 90% of orders. Bespoke would have killed the platform.

✓ Said yes

Ship MMR before lawnmower.

Founders preferred lawnmower (bigger TAM, sexier demo). I argued indoor first — shorter sales cycle, faster iteration. Took 2 months to land.
