"Technical credibility is a PM superpower."
How I work
01

Buildable PRDs.

I read ROS logs and approve schematics. My specs survive contact with engineering — and engineering trusts me when I say "we're shipping this."

02

Customer site is the source of truth.

I've shipped at customer factories on three continents. My roadmap reflects what works on the plant floor — not what reads well in a deck.

03

Decisions over features.

I keep a decision log. I'd rather say no in week 1 than ship the wrong thing in week 12. The hardest call I've made was holding L&T to three standard payload trims when procurement wanted bespoke.
